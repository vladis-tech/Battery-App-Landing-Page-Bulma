Frequently Ask Questions

1. How does the Optimizing Battery app extend my battery life?

The app analyzes your usage patterns and adjusts background activity, screen settings, and app refresh rates to reduce unnecessary power consumption and extend battery life.

2. Will the app slow down my phone while optimizing the battery?

No, the app is designed to run efficiently in the background. It only targets non-essential processes, ensuring your phone stays fast and responsive.

3. Can I customize which apps the optimizer manages?

Yes, you can whitelist or blacklist apps within the settings, giving you full control over which apps are optimized or left untouched.

4. Does the app work automatically, or do I need to start it manually?

The app offers both options. You can let it run automatically in the background or manually activate optimization whenever needed.
